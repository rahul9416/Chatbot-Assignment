# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What does Techculture do?

### Where is Techculture located?

We have offices in Bangalore, India and San Francisco, USA. However, we work with clients globally and our teams operate across multiple time zones.

## Project & Engagement Questions

### What is your onboarding process?

Our onboarding process follows these steps:

1. **Initial Consultation** (Free): 30-minute discovery call to understand your requirements
2. **Proposal & Estimate**: We prepare a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and cost estimate within 3–5 business days
3. **Contract & Kickoff**: Once agreed, we sign the contract, collect the initial payment, and schedule a kickoff meeting
4. **Team Assignment**: We assemble the right team based on your project needs
5. **Project Start**: Development begins with weekly sprint cycles and regular updates

### How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

- Simple projects (landing pages, basic APIs): 2–6 weeks
- Medium projects (web apps, chatbots, ML models): 6–12 weeks
- Complex projects (enterprise platforms, large-scale AI systems): 12–24 weeks
  We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

### Do you work with startups or only enterprise clients?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from early-stage startups building their MVP to Fortune 500 enterprises running large-scale digital transformation programs. Our pricing models are flexible to accommodate different budgets.

### Can I start with a small project to test your capabilities?

Absolutely! Many of our long-term clients started with a pilot project or proof-of-concept. We encourage this approach as it builds trust and helps us understand your business better.

## Security & Compliance

### Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we are happy to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements before discussing project details. We take data confidentiality very seriously and can accommodate custom NDA requirements.

### How do you handle sensitive data?

We follow strict data handling protocols:

- All team members undergo security awareness training
- Data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access is granted on a need-to-know basis
- We comply with GDPR, SOC 2, and HIPAA requirements when applicable
- We never use client data for any purpose beyond the project scope

## Communication & Support

### How do you communicate during a project?

We use the following communication channels:

- **Daily standups:** 15-minute sync via Slack or Microsoft Teams
- **Weekly demos:** Video call to showcase progress and gather feedback
- **Project management:** Jira or Trello for task tracking and transparency
- **Documentation:** Confluence or Notion for shared documentation

### What happens after the project is delivered?

We offer post-delivery support options:

- **30-day warranty:** Free bug fixes for 30 days after delivery
- **Maintenance contract:** Monthly retainer for ongoing support, updates, and minor enhancements
- **Ad-hoc support:** Hourly support for specific issues or changes

### What if I need to scale the team up or down?

Our dedicated team model is flexible. You can scale the team size with 2 weeks' notice. We can also adjust the skill mix based on project phase (e.g., more developers during build, more QA during testing).
