## Payment Terms

- **Milestone-based:** 20% upfront, remainder split across project milestones
- **Monthly retainers:** Billed at the start of each month
- **Hourly:** Billed bi-weekly based on tracked hours
- **Net-30 payment terms** for enterprise clients with established relationships

## Discounts

- 10% discount for contracts longer than 6 months
- 15% discount for nonprofits and educational institutions
- Volume discounts available for large-scale engagements

## Note

All prices are in USD. Final pricing depends on project scope, complexity, timeline, and specific technical requirements. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your needs.
