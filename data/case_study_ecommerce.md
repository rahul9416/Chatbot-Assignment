# Case Study: Digital Marketing Transformation for ShopNova (E-Commerce)

## Client

**ShopNova** — An online fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform with 200,000+ active customers and $15M in annual revenue.

## Industry

E-Commerce / Retail

## Challenge

ShopNova was struggling with declining organic traffic (-15% year-over-year), high customer acquisition costs ($45 per customer), and poor email marketing performance (12% open rate, 1.2% CTR). Their marketing team of 3 needed a comprehensive digital strategy overhaul to compete with larger players in the market.

## Solution

Techculture implemented a full-stack digital marketing transformation:

### 1. SEO & Content Strategy

- Conducted comprehensive technical SEO audit and fixed 200+ issues (broken links, slow page speed, missing meta tags, duplicate content)
- Developed a keyword strategy targeting 500+ long-tail keywords in fashion and lifestyle niches
- Created a content calendar with 12 blog posts per month, including style guides, trend reports, and product comparisons
- Implemented schema markup for product pages, improving rich snippet appearance in search results

### 2. PPC Campaign Optimization

- Audited and restructured Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns
- Implemented dynamic product ads on Facebook and Instagram
- Set up Google Shopping campaigns with optimized product feeds
- Created retargeting campaigns for cart abandoners and past purchasers
- Implemented conversion tracking and attribution modeling

### 3. Email Marketing Automation

- Migrated from basic email blasts to segmented, automated workflows using Klaviyo
- Built 8 automated email sequences: welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back, product recommendations, birthday, VIP loyalty, and re-engagement
- Implemented dynamic product recommendations based on browsing and purchase history
- A/B tested subject lines, send times, and content layouts

### 4. Social Media Growth

- Developed brand-consistent content for Instagram, Pinterest, and TikTok
- Partnered with 25 micro-influencers for product showcases and reviews
- Created user-generated content campaigns (#ShopNovaStyle) that drove organic engagement
- Managed community engagement and customer interactions across all platforms

### 5. Analytics Dashboard

- Built a custom analytics dashboard in Looker Studio integrating Google Analytics 4, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and Klaviyo data
- Set up real-time KPI tracking: ROAS, CPA, organic traffic, email revenue, social engagement
- Created automated weekly and monthly performance reports

## Technologies & Platforms Used

- **SEO:** SEMrush, Screaming Frog, Google Search Console, Ahrefs
- **PPC:** Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, Google Merchant Center
- **Email:** Klaviyo, with custom HTML templates
- **Social:** Later (scheduling), Canva (design), Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4, Looker Studio, Google Tag Manager
