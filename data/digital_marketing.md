# Digital Marketing Services

## Overview

Techculture's digital marketing division combines creative strategy with data-driven execution. We help businesses grow their online presence, acquire customers, and maximize ROI through targeted campaigns across all major digital channels.

## Service Offerings

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

We improve your website's visibility on search engines through technical SEO, on-page optimization, content strategy, and link building. Our SEO campaigns typically deliver 30–50% organic traffic growth within 3 months.

- **Technical SEO:** Site speed optimization, mobile responsiveness, schema markup, crawl budget management
- **Content SEO:** Keyword research, content gap analysis, blog strategy, pillar page creation
- **Off-Page SEO:** Quality backlink building, digital PR, guest posting
- **Timeline:** Ongoing monthly retainer (minimum 3 months recommended)

### Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising

We create and manage high-performance ad campaigns on Google Ads, Bing Ads, and social media platforms. Our PPC specialists optimize for conversion while minimizing cost-per-acquisition.

- **Platforms:** Google Search & Display, YouTube Ads, Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram), LinkedIn Ads
- **Services:** Campaign setup, ad copy creation, A/B testing, bid management, conversion tracking
- **Average Results:** 25% reduction in CPA within the first 2 months

### Social Media Marketing

We build and execute social media strategies that grow your brand presence and engage your audience. Our team handles content creation, community management, influencer partnerships, and paid social campaigns.

- **Platforms:** Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok
- **Deliverables:** Content calendar, branded graphics, engagement reports, growth analytics
- **Timeline:** Ongoing monthly retainer

### Content Marketing

We create compelling content that attracts, educates, and converts your target audience. From blog posts and whitepapers to video scripts and infographics, our content team delivers high-quality, SEO-optimized content.

- **Content Types:** Blog articles, whitepapers, case studies, email newsletters, video scripts, landing pages
- **Process:** Strategy → Research → Creation → Review → Publish → Analyze
- **Output:** 8–12 content pieces per month (varies by package)

### Email Marketing & Automation

We design email campaigns and automated workflows that nurture leads and drive conversions. Our email strategies achieve industry-leading open rates and click-through rates.

- **Services:** Campaign design, segmentation, A/B testing, drip sequences, re-engagement campaigns
- **Tools:** HubSpot, Mailchimp, SendGrid, Klaviyo
- **Average Results:** 20–30% open rate, 3–5% click-through rate

### Analytics & Reporting

We set up comprehensive analytics dashboards that give you real-time visibility into your marketing performance. Track KPIs, measure ROI, and make data-driven decisions.

- **Tools:** Google Analytics 4, Looker Studio, HubSpot Analytics, custom dashboards
- **Deliverables:** Weekly performance reports, monthly strategy reviews, quarterly ROI analysis
